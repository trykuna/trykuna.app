Features that just work

Every feature is designed to help you stay focused and get things done — without getting in your way.

Calendar Sync

See tasks alongside your schedule. Kuna keeps everything in sync with your calendar.

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. Connect to your own self-hosted Vikunja with no tracking or hidden collection.

Views that fit you

Choose how you want to see your tasks — list, grouped, or by project. Kuna adapts to the way you work.

Background Sync

Stay up to date automatically. Kuna keeps your tasks current across devices.

Native iOS Experience

Built with SwiftUI for iOS 18.4+. Feels completely at home with iOS design patterns and system features.

Widgets

Check your tasks at a glance with iOS Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets. Stay on top of your day without opening the app.

Frequently asked questions

Vikunja is an open-source, self-hosted task management platform. Kuna connects to your Vikunja instance to sync tasks while keeping your data private and under your control.

Kuna uses EventKit to bidirectionally sync your tasks with your iOS calendar. Tasks with due dates appear as calendar events, and changes in either app are reflected in both places.

Kuna requires iOS 18.4 or later. It's built with SwiftUI and takes advantage of the latest iOS features for the best experience.
